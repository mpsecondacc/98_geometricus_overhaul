About us

About us

The company Geometricus d.o.o. was founded in 2011 with its headquarters in Ferovac. It soon achieves significant growth in the turnover of services and the number of employees. The company's employees are master engineers in geodesy / geoinformatics and geodetic technicians who perform work tasks at the highest possible level and thus contribute to the development and reputation of the company.

We operate in accordance with regulations with the consent of the State geodetic administration for the performance of state surveying and real estate cadastre work and the authorization of Croatia's chamber of authorized geodesy engineers.

Surveying services

The main activities of the company are services related to real estate cadastre and engineering geodesy. Among other activities, we provide services in the field of photogrammetry, terrestrial laser scanning, LiDAR scanning, hydrographic surveying, locating underground utilities. We also offer services related to the creation of geoinformation systems and their web-hosting.

Mission

The mission of Geometricus is the satisfaction of every client by providing services from all areas of our activities using the highest quality and most modern solutions for their projects and thus contributing to the development and progress of the company.

Vision

By using the knowledge and experience of our employees and by constantly investing in top-notch equipment and the training of employees, we will become a recognized and reliable partner on the regional market. It is also very important for us to remain a socially responsible company and to become a desirable employer for both current and potential employees.
